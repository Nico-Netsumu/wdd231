Site Plan

Project for Nature Explorer Website

Site Name

Nature Explorer

This name was selected to reflect the website’s focus on outdoor nature trails, wildlife, and ecological adventures. It’s simple, easy to remember, and broad enough for future expansion.

Optional domain: natureexplorer.net

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a resource for nature lovers, offering detailed guides on hiking trails, native wildlife, and eco-tour activities. It will include interactive maps, trail recommendations, and a community blog.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find beginner-friendly nature trails in my region?
  • What equipment do I need for a weekend eco-hiking trip?

Color Scheme

The chosen color scheme reflects a natural and calming aesthetic:

Primary
Accent
  • Primary Color (#2d6a4f): Used for headings, navigation, and accents.
  • Accent Color (#95d5b2): Used for buttons, highlights, and background elements.

Typography

  • Montserrat: Used for main headings to give a modern, clean look.
  • Open Sans: Used for body text for readability and accessibility.
